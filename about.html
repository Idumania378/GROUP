Empowering students. Inspiring futures.

Citam Schools is a leading educational institution in East and Central Africa, dedicated to providing quality education for students. With a focus on nurturing the potential of each student, we offer a range of academic, extracurricular, and pastoral programs aimed at holistic development.

Experienced Teachers

Our highly qualified and experienced teachers are passionate about education and use innovative teaching methods to inspire and engage students.

Parent Collaboration

We believe in working collaboratively with parents to create a supportive and enriching learning environment for our students.

Holistic Development

We emphasize the holistic development of our students by offering a wide range of extracurricular activities and focusing on their social, emotional, and physical well-being.


Comprehensive Curriculum

Our school offers a comprehensive curriculum that incorporates both academic and co-curricular activities, providing students with a well-rounded education.

Individualized Attention

We understand the importance of individualized attention in promoting student growth. Our small class sizes allow teachers to personalize learning experiences for every student.

Advanced Facilities

Citam Schools boasts state-of-the-art facilities that enhance the learning experience. From well-equipped classrooms to modern sports facilities, we provide students with the resources they need to excel.
